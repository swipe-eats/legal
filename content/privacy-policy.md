This Privacy Policy explains how Swipe Eats collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects personal information when you use our mobile application, websites, web pages, services, features, and related tools collectively referred to as the "Service."

By using Swipe Eats, you acknowledge the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.


## 1. Introduction

Swipe Eats is a recipe discovery and social food platform. Users can create accounts, post recipes, upload photos or videos, create collections, follow other users, like, save, comment, search, and interact with recipes and profiles.

Swipe Eats may make User Content available through websites, public web pages, shared links, previews, search results, and other online surfaces.

Because Swipe Eats includes social features, recommendations, search, analytics, advertising, web publishing, and personalized discovery, we collect information needed to operate the app and website, show user content, improve recommendations, maintain security, analyze usage, measure performance, and provide support.

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect through the Service, including the Swipe Eats app, websites, public web pages, and related online features.
